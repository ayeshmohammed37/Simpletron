Use a sentinel-controlled loop to read positive integers, then compute and print 
their sum.

**Program structure**
>intialize sum to zero
>read number
>while number not negative
	>add number to sum
	>read number
>print sum


**Program Instructions(accumulator{0} by default)**
loc   word  ins
 00 > 2150 (store sum)
 01 > 1051 (read num) 
 02 > 2051 (load num)
 03 > 4106 (branch neg to 07) // print sum and end program
 04 > 3050 (add sum)
 05 > 4000 (branch to 00)
 06 > 1150 (write sum)
 07 > 4300 (halt)
